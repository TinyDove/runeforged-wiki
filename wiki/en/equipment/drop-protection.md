# Dropped Equipment Protection

Equipment with Runeforged quality, affixes, or a special variant remains on the ground longer after being dropped.

| Equipment state | Ground lifetime |
| --- | ---: |
| Legendary | 60 minutes |
| Rare, Alien-Cursed, or carrying an Infusion Affix | 30 minutes |
| Magic, other Cursed equipment, any other Runeforged equipment, or a special weapon variant | 10 minutes |

Ordinary items use Minecraft's standard despawn time. Protected lifetime is saved with the item and is not reset by chunk unloading or server restarts. Hostile mobs cannot pick up protected equipment; other item-moving entities and mechanics retain their normal behavior.
