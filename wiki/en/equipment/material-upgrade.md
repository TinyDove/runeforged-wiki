# Equipment Material Upgrade

Material Upgrade moves an established non-Cursed weapon or armor piece to a higher material while retaining the target item's Runeforged properties.

## Smithing Table Slots

| Template | Base | Addition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Reforging Stone | Equipment to upgrade | Higher-material blank weapon of the same family or armor of the same slot |

The addition must be blank: Common quality, with no Runeforged data, variant, Rune archetype, pending affix roll, custom name, or enchantments.

## Weapon Upgrade

Supported families are Sword, Axe, Spear, Dagger, Rapier, Greatsword, and Scythe. The addition must belong to the same family and have a higher material rank.

Weapon order:

`Wood / Gold → Stone → Copper → Iron → Diamond → Netherite`

Wood and Gold share a rank and cannot upgrade into one another.

## Armor Upgrade

Standard Helmets, Chestplates, Leggings, and Boots are supported. The addition must use the same equipment slot.

Armor order:

`Leather → Copper → Chainmail → Gold / Iron → Diamond → Netherite`

Gold and Iron share a rank and cannot upgrade into one another; Gold can move only to Diamond or Netherite. Turtle Shells and Elytra are outside this standard armor upgrade system.

## Retained Properties and Restrictions

- The target retains quality, regular affixes, Legendary Affix, Sealed Affixes, Infusion, Transmutation, special weapon variant, and other Runeforged data.
- The resulting item type and base attributes come from the higher-material addition.
- The result uses the blank addition's durability state.
- Cursed equipment cannot be upgraded.
- Only movement to a higher material is allowed; same-rank conversion and downgrading are not supported.
