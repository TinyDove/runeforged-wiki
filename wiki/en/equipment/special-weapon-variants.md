# Special Weapon Variants

A special weapon variant replaces one or more fixed family traits and retains every family trait it does not replace. “Final fixed traits” below is the complete set active on that weapon and does not consume regular-affix slots.

Material determines base Attack Damage, base Attack Speed, and durability, so the tables list only additional Runeforged traits.

## Obtaining

Generated monster weapons have a base `10%` variant chance, plus `2.5` percentage points per Elite Affix. Chest and fishing weapon rewards have a `25%` chance.

## Material Reforging

At a Smithing Table, use a Reforging Stone, a variant weapon, and a higher-tier blank weapon of the same family to transfer the variant to the new material. The new base must be Common and have no affixes, variant, custom name, or enchantments. Cursed variants are ineligible.

Material order: Wood/Gold → Stone → Copper → Iron → Diamond → Netherite. Runeforged properties and the variant are retained; durability state is recalculated for the new base.

## Flat-Damage Coefficients

A Variant coefficient adjustment is added directly to its Weapon Family's base coefficient. It affects only Physical/Elemental flat-damage Affixes and the flat portion of Compound Damage; it does not affect percentage values, Affix names, Tiers, or count. For example, Hand Axe uses the Axe coefficient `1.25 - 0.05 = 1.20`.

Short Scythe uses an override: its Physical coefficient is fixed at `1.00`, while Elemental flat damage retains the Scythe's `1.00`. See [Weapon Base Stats](../reference/weapon-base-stats.md#flat-damage-coefficients) for family coefficients and the complete calculation rule.
