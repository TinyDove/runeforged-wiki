# Curses & Infusion

## Cursed Equipment

Cursed equipment cannot use ordinary forging currency, enchanting, or repair. Generated Cursed equipment generally contains regular affixes, extra curse-area affixes, and one negative curse; Sealed, Alien-Cursed, and Rune-Cursed equipment use their own structures.

A Sealed Gem can turn Rare equipment into Sealed Cursed equipment or reroll an eligible curse area and negative curse.

## Alienation

Alienation preserves the item itself and applies several random changes. It may fill or replace regular affixes, change a Tier, reroll values, distort a highest-Tier affix, empower Infusion, or add an Alien-specific effect.

Common, Magic, and Rare equipment first fills regular affixes according to its existing count, then receives the random changes.

## Infusion

Non-Common equipment can accumulate Infusion Value from kills. At 100%, it receives a separate Infusion Affix; weapons normally receive T2 and armor T3.

Normal, Sealed, and Alien-Cursed equipment stops after its first Infusion Affix. Nature Rune-Cursed Weapons can repeat the cycle.
