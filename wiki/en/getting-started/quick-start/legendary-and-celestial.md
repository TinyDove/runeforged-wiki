# Legendary & Celestial Forging

## Legendary Equipment

An Ascension Stone turns Common, Magic, or Rare equipment Legendary: it fills five regular affixes and adds one T1 Legendary Affix. Legendary weapons also have fixed All Damage, while Legendary armor has fixed All Damage Reduction.

A Celestial Fragment rerolls the Legendary Affix without changing regular affixes or other special properties.

## Regular T0 Affixes

A regular affix that has T1 can rarely break through to T0. It keeps the original affix name and eligibility rules, occupies a regular-affix slot, and has a fixed value equal to `120%` of that affix's maximum T1 value.

Regular T0 can appear on equipment from non-crafted sources. A Celestial Tempering Stone has a `10%` chance to attempt regular T0 for its new affix; a Celestial Penance Stone has a `5%` chance for its replacement. Alien-specific and Legendary Affixes are not regular T0 affixes in this section.

## Celestial Currency

Celestial versions provide stronger, more certain, or more focused forms of their base currency. A Celestial Tempering Stone adds at least T1, a Celestial Sealed Gem creates three curse-area affixes with at least one T1, and a Celestial Infusion Gem performs a direct stronger Infusion.

All Celestial currency differences and recipes are listed together on the Equipment Currency page.
