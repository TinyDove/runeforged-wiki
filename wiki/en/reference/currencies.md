# Equipment Currency Reference

See [Equipment Currency](../equipment/currency-workflow.md) for complete conditions and outcomes.

| Currency | Effect | Main restriction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Shaping Stone | Turns Common equipment Magic and adds its first affix | Common equipment only |
| Reinforcement Stone | Adds one affix, up to three | Equipment with 1–2 regular affixes |
| Tempering Stone | Adds one affix; becomes Rare at four | Not Cursed or already full |
| Reforging Stone | Clears Runeforged forging and repairs durability | Not Legendary, Cursed, or carrying Sealed Affixes |
| Stripping Stone | Removes one random regular affix | At least one regular affix |
| Penance Stone | Replaces a lowest-Tier regular affix | At least one regular affix |
| Edict Stone | Rerolls regular-affix values | Preserves names, Tiers, and count |
| Ascension Stone | Makes equipment Legendary and fills affixes | Common, Magic, or Rare equipment |
| Celestial Fragment | Rerolls a Legendary Affix | Legendary or Mythic equipment with one; Mythic remains maximum-value T0 |
| Sealed Gem | Creates Sealed Cursed equipment or rerolls its curse area | Eligible Rare, Normal Cursed, or Sealed Cursed equipment |
| Infusion Gem | Adds infusion progress or rerolls an Infusion Affix | Non-Common equipment eligible for infusion |
| Alien Gem | Creates Alien-Cursed equipment and applies random changes | See the Alien Gem page |

Rune Fragments are crafting ingredients and cannot be applied directly to equipment.
