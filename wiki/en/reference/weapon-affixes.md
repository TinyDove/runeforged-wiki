### Blessing Conditions

| Blessing | Condition |
| --- | --- |
| Blessing of the Sun | Overworld daytime, game time `0–12299` |
| Blessing of Shadows | Overworld night, game time `13000–22999` |
| Blessing of Storms | Attacker in water, or Overworld rain in a biome with precipitation |
| Blessing of Otherworlds | Any non-Overworld dimension |

Sun and Shadows are both inactive during the short transition gaps. The in-water Storm condition works in every dimension and can overlap Otherworlds; if special equipment carries both active Blessings, their values add.

### Slayer Targets

| Affix | Targets |
| --- | --- |
| Undead Slayer | Entities in Minecraft's `#minecraft:undead` tag |
| Human Slayer | Players; Villagers and Wandering Traders; Witches, Pillagers, Vindicators, Evokers, Illusioners; Piglins and Piglin Brutes; Zombie Villagers and Zombified Piglins; Endermen and Blazes |
| Giant Slayer | Bounding-box height at least `2.0` blocks, or a Runeforged Giant variant |

If several regular Slayer classes match, only the highest percentage is used. Zombie Villagers and Zombified Piglins can match both Undead and Human.

Against a Giant variant, independent multipliers also apply: Giant Slayer `×2.0`; a matching Undead Slayer `×1.5`; a matching Human Slayer `×1.5`. Multiple matches multiply.

## Conflicts

| Group | Rule |
| --- | --- |
| Slayer | Undead, Human, and Giant Slayer conflict |
| Physical conversion | The four Physical-to-element affixes conflict |
| Blessing | The four Blessings conflict |
| Special melee damage | Leap, Dash, Weapon Skill, and Extra Sweep conflict |
| Fire/Frost | Fire flat, compound, and conversion conflict with their Frost counterparts |
| Lightning/Holy | Lightning flat, compound, and conversion conflict with their Holy counterparts |
| Additional | Sun conflicts with Frost side; Shadows with Holy side; Storms with Fire side; Otherworlds with Lightning side |

Elemental Resistance Penetration can roll only after the weapon already has elemental flat, compound, or conversion damage.

## Weapon Restrictions

- Greatswords cannot roll Attack Speed.
- Daggers cannot roll Attack Range; Daggers and Rapiers cannot roll Knockback Distance.
- Scythes cannot roll Leap Attack Damage.
- Rapiers, Daggers, Axes, Spears, Tridents, and Maces cannot roll Extra Sweep.
- Hoes, Pickaxes, Shovels, and other tools cannot roll Weapon Skill, Leap, Dash, Extra Sweep, or Single-Wield Damage.
- Tridents cannot roll Fire flat, Fire Compound, or Physical-to-Fire; they can only roll Blessing of Storms and cannot roll Dash or Extra Sweep.
- Maces cannot roll Weapon Skill, Dash, or Extra Sweep.

Generated Rare and Legendary weapons are guaranteed at least one weapon damage affix. Weapon Skill Damage affects Weapon Skill attacks only.
